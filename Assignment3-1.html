THE SPRING SEASON

When the new leaves emerge for the trees, when buds play their magic, when butterflies and eyebrows decorate them, it is the onset of spring season. It is considered as the most beautiful season in the country.

THE SUMMER SEASON

The temperatures in this season are very high i.e the weather is very hot. The days are long during this period, while the nights are short. The average temperature hovers around 311.15 Kelvin.

THE RAINY SEASON

From the end of summer, it starts raining across entire India which indicates the start of Rainy or Monsoon season. This season help the farmers very much. Water bodies like lakes, rivers are filled with water during this times.

THE MOONLIT SEASON

This season is known as Sharad Ritu in Sanskrit. This is the season just after the rainy season. The sky looks very beautiful during night times of this season. This season starts from Aswayuga Masa and lasts till Karthika Masa.

THE WINTER SEASON

The Winter season is the coldest season of the year, usually seen between the months of December and February. The average temperature settels at 296.15 Kelvin. It is the time for some important harvest festival of South-India i.e MAKAR SANKRANTHI.

THE AUTUMN SEASON

The leaves start falling off the trees in this season, so it is also known as the fall season. The leaves turn yellow before falling down which give a new beautiful look to the trees.
